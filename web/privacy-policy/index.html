Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the CFPusher Chrome extension. By using this extension, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect data to improve your experience with CFPusher. This includes:

  • Codeforces Submissions: We fetch your Codeforces submissions to track and sync accepted solutions to your GitHub repositories.
  • GitHub Authentication Data: We collect and store your GitHub authentication tokens temporarily to interact with your repositories.

The data collected is stored securely and is only used for the functionality of the extension.

How We Use Your Information

The data we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Automatically push accepted Codeforces submissions to your linked GitHub repository.
  • Track your Codeforces streaks and display problem-solving statistics in the extension.
  • Manage the integration between Codeforces and GitHub, ensuring smooth functionality.

Cookies and Tracking

CFPusher does not use cookies for tracking purposes. However, we use browser local storage to temporarily store GitHub authentication data required for the extension to function. This information is never sold or shared with third parties.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Third-Party Services

CFPusher integrates with third-party services including Codeforces and GitHub for authentication and data synchronization. These services maintain their own privacy policies and practices. We encourage you to review them separately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and the "Last updated" date will be revised accordingly.
